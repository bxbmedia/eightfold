    # Product Requirements Document (PRD)

    ## Project Title

    Volt Electric Vehicle Detailing ‚Äì Marketing Landing Page

    ## Author

    Krystian Molina

    ## Purpose

    This PRD defines the requirements for building a single-page marketing landing page for a fictional mobile electric vehicle (EV) detailing business. The document is intended to be used by an AI collaborator (e.g., Claude) to guide design, development, and iteration within a short, exploratory build window.

    The goal is not perfection, but rapid experimentation, learning effective AI collaboration patterns, and producing a polished, functional prototype.

    ---

    ## Background & Context

    Volt is a mobile vehicle detailing service specializing exclusively in electric vehicles. The brand emphasizes modern aesthetics, EV-safe products, and a waterless wash technique. Customers are tech-savvy EV owners (primarily Tesla drivers) who are likely browsing on mobile devices, sometimes directly from their cars.

    This project is a sandbox exercise with an approximate build time of one hour.
